Welcome to Patmos Inn

Patmos Inn | Seafront Heritage Residence in Skala Patmos Greece

Patmos Inn is a luxury seafront heritage residence in Skala Patmos, Greece, where refined island living meets understated elegance. Set on Saint Theologos Beach, the property enjoys a privileged location just steps from the Marina and only a 2-minute walk from the port and the lively center of Skala.

Designed for discerning travelers seeking privacy, comfort, and authentic Aegean ambiance, Patmos Inn features a spectacular 52 sq.m. sea-view terrace and balcony overlooking the Aegean Sea, the Marina, the port, and the iconic Monastery of Saint John the Theologian in Chora.

The 124 sq.m. residence accommodates up to 6 guests across 3 elegant bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and a guest WC. Interiors include a sophisticated open-plan living area, a fully equipped contemporary kitchen, and a dedicated sea-view workspace with high-speed Wi-Fi — ideal for luxury holidays with family and friends, extended stays, and effortless remote living by the sea.

Combining beachfront living, panoramic sea views, heritage character, and walkable access to cafés, restaurants, boutiques, and the Marina, Patmos Inn offers one of the most exclusive stays in the heart of Skala Patmos.

Heritage Architecture Restored for Contemporary Island Living

Originally built in 1920, Patmos Inn once served as a visual landmark helping guide ships safely into Skala Harbor away from Kenepsis Reef.

Its distinctive double-framed tiled roof remains an architectural rarity on Patmos, originally designed to collect rainwater while protecting the residence from seasonal winds and strong summer light.

Today, the residence has been carefully restored by its fourth-generation owner, preserving original stone walls, handcrafted wooden openings, and traditional Patmian proportions while introducing refined contemporary comfort.

Natural textures, warm woods, soft fabrics, and sea-oriented interiors create an atmosphere that feels timeless, calm, and deeply connected to the landscape of Patmos.

The Experience

Life at Patmos Inn unfolds slowly beside the water. Begin each morning with coffee overlooking the harbor and a swim moments from the residence. Spend long afternoons between the shaded waterfront terrace and the rhythm of Skala’s harbor life just outside your door.

As evening arrives, the marina lights and illuminated Monastery create one of the island’s most atmospheric nighttime views.

An increasingly rare way to experience Patmos beside the waterfront.
