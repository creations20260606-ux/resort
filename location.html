LOCATION

Walk Everywhere from the Waterfront

Patmos Inn is located in the heart of Skala, Patmos, Greece, above Saint Theologos Beach, steps from the Marina and the historic 95 A.D. Baptistery of Saint John the Theologian. Set above a small beachfront café, Patmos Inn enjoys a rare balance of privacy, sea proximity, and authentic island rhythm.

Direct visual access to the marina makes Patmos Inn ideal for yacht travelers. The residence combines panoramic waterfront views, walkable harbor living, architectural authenticity, and immediate access to the island’s restaurants, cafés, boutiques, and port.

Guests experience the atmosphere of Patmos directly beside the Aegean while remaining fully connected to the rhythm of island life.

Discover Patmos

Patmos is one of Greece’s most refined and discreet island destinations, known for its spiritual heritage, preserved architecture, sophisticated atmosphere, and understated luxury.

Home to the UNESCO-listed Monastery of Saint John the Theologian and the Cave of the Apocalypse, the island attracts travelers seeking authenticity, culture, privacy, and a slower Mediterranean rhythm away from mass tourism.

Its unique combination of yacht culture, elegant simplicity, protected architecture, and harbor life has established Patmos as one of the Mediterranean’s most quietly exclusive destinations.
